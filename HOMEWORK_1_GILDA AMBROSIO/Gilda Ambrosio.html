Gilda Ambrosio

"The more we become proud of our diversity, the more we will be aware of being unique. "

She was born in Naples in 1992, she grew up surrounded by fashion in her parents' boutique, and enjoyed dressing up the shop's mannequins because, as a chubby child, she says she felt self-conscious about wearing them herself.
